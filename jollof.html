The Ingredients needed

  • 5 cups of rice
  • 3 cubes of nuchicken magi, salt, chicken oil, tomatoe paste, red pepper and tomatoes
  • red meat, chicken drumstick, stock fish, and shrimps
  • salad leaves and cabbage

Preparation procedures

  1. put the pot on a low-medium gas cooker. Perboil the rice untill it becomes clean and neat. Pour the rice on fire
  2. Blend the pepper, the tomatoes together. Mix the blended ingredients with the tomatoe paste. Pour them inside the rice
  3. Add the chicken drumstick, red meat, stock fish and shrimps all at the same time
  4. Wait for about 10 mins, then add the nuchicken magi and salt to taste. Cover and leave it on fire for at least 30 mins or untill its done. Serve and enjoy the yummy food
